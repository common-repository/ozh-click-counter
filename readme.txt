How many people click on your links ? Track clicks: link popularity, download conter, etc...

== Description ==

You have a blog. You have stats telling you how many people read it. You post links. But do you have something telling you how many people click on your links? Count clicks on your files (download counter) or on any links.

For documentation and tips, please refer to the official plugin page for [Click Counter](http://planetozh.com/blog/archives/2004/09/17/click-counter-plugin-for-wordpress/ "Click Counter")

== Installation ==

Installation:

1. Upload wp_ozh_clickcounter.php to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Upload go.php to your blog root (where wp-config.php is)
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Refer to the official plugin page for documentation, usage and tips
